SPEAK THE TRUTH

Project description: "Now is the Time" to speak the truth. Nowadays, no one knows what to believe in the world. Especially when on every news platform of social media, fake news is being displayed. My project is going to show these fake stories through animation and give awareness to everyone to not tell lies or spread these fake news stories.

  • TV Screen (awareness commercial on young adult channels)
    • It is a TV screen that you can walk in front of and on the side. You can get as close as you want to it. It is a flatscreen TV. It is a living room TV in normal peoples houses. The sound from both the TV and living family in the house is loud.
  • Phone Screen
    • It is a young adults phone. It is a pop up animation when scrolling on social media. The sound is quiet if not using headphones. You can touch it.
  • Computer Screen
    • It is a laptop. It cannot be touched. You can walk around the screen. The sound is quiet. It can be seen on social media pages or youtube ads.
